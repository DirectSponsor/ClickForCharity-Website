Site Map

Every page of Click for Charity is listed here so crawlers and visitors can find the right spot quickly.

Public Pages

  • Home – Overview of the mission and how the project works.
  • Surf Ads – Primary reward experience where members view ads and earn tokens.
  • Simple Tasks – Low-effort tasks that refresh frequently for more ways to earn.
  • Skipped Tasks – Review and retry any tasks you skipped earlier.
  • Surveys – Coming soon notice for the survey experience.
  • Offers – Preview for the offer wall being built.

Member & Admin Tools

  • Profile – Account status and upcoming profile features.
  • Admin Dashboard – Central admin hub for managing content.
  • Admin – Surf Ads Management – Create and maintain surf ad tasks.
  • Admin – Simple Tasks – Control simple task campaigns (mock data).
  • Admin Analytics – Quick stats and navigation shortcuts.
  • Admin Legacy – Archive dashboard used for earlier experiments.

Support Pages

  • Thank You – Gratitude page with animated hero and timer.
  • Hero Cover Image – Shared image used in open graph cards.
