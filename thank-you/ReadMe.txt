Easy to update sections

To change ads (top section):
Look for the ads-container div - you can swap out the three ad items inside.

To change tasks (bottom section):
Look for the tasks-container div - you can update the three task cards.

To adjust timer duration:
In the JavaScript, change let timeLeft = 10; to whatever number you want.
