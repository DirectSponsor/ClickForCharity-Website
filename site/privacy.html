Privacy at Click for Charity: We Don't Care Who You Are (And That's the Point)

No tracking, no leaks. We analyzed the site‚Äîit's pure static HTML with zero JavaScript, no external trackers (Google Analytics? Nope.), no cookies, no sneaky third-party requests. No forms on the main page harvesting anything. Your browser loads plain content, that's it. No data leaks because there's nothing collecting or phoning home.

How We Handle Ads (Now and Maybe Later)

It's low-threat by design‚Äîeasy to block with uBlock Origin if you want‚Äîand aligns with not caring who you are.
