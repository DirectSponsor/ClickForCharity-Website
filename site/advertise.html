Advertise on Click for Charity

Your ad spend doesn't go to shareholders — it funds real people's projects. Every dollar we earn from advertising is allocated to a specific recipient project: buying equipment, covering costs, or reaching a funding target.

We have two ad positions, each with 10 slots. Each slot represents 10% of impressions on that position. You choose how many slots you want — that's your share of the audience.

Where the money goes

Ad revenue is allocated to specific recipient projects — things like tools, materials, or equipment that people need to improve their situation. The project and its target are visible on the site. When a target is reached, the project is marked complete and a new one begins.

There is no time pressure on our end. If traffic is lower than target, the project just takes longer to fund. You pay for the slots you get, not a guaranteed impression count.

FAQ

What if someone else already has slots on my chosen position?
You share the rotation. If they have 3 slots and you buy 4, you get 40% of impressions and they get 30%. Up to 10 slots total per position.
What happens to unused slots?
We fill them with our own house ads — reflinks and promotions for the network. The first advertiser on a position automatically gets all unused slots until others buy in.
Can I get a discount?
Early advertisers can negotiate a reduced rate. We'd rather have a real ad running at a lower price than an empty slot. Get in touch.
What ad formats are accepted?
Any valid HTML — a plain image, a linked image, or a third-party ad network script tag. We review all ads before activation.
How is payment handled?
We generate a Lightning invoice for the agreed amount in sats, calculated at the current BTC/USD rate. You pay once and the ad runs for the agreed period.
Can I renew?
Yes. We'll contact you before your ad expires. Early adopters who renew keep their original slot rate.
