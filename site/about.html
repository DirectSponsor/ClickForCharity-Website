Data Synchronization

ClickForCharity uses a hybrid system to keep your data safe and synchronized across all DirectSponsor sites:

üí∞ Balance (Instant)

Your balance is managed centrally on our secure auth server and updates in real-time across all sites. No manual sync needed - your earnings are available immediately on any DirectSponsor site.

üìÅ Profile & Tasks (Automatic)

Your profile settings, completed tasks, and platform preferences are stored locally and synchronized automatically via secure file sync. This happens in the background - no action required from you.
