About Click for Charity

Support Projects Through Action

Real Impact, Real People

We support individuals working toward financial independence through their own projects. These aren't faceless organizations - they're people building businesses, learning skills, and creating lasting change in their communities.

3-Year Goal: Each recipient aims to become self-sufficient through regular support while they build their future. Your contributions provide the stable income they need to focus on their path to independence.

Why Bitcoin?

We use Bitcoin Lightning for fast, low-cost payments that maximize the amount reaching recipients. No traditional banking fees, no delays - just efficient support that goes where it's needed most.

Transparent: Every transaction is visible on the blockchain. Efficient: Near-instant payments with minimal fees. Global: Support projects anywhere in the world.
